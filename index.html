About

Currently pursuing PhD in the Electronics and Telecommunication (ETCE) Engineering Department of Jadavpur University (JU), Kolkata. I work in the Imaging, Vision and Pattern Recognition (IVPR) Group led by my guide- Dr. Ananda S. Chowhdury . Researching on developing better detection, classification, segmentation and prediction algorithms for various brain disorders using various imaging data (radiological, histopathological, etc.) and phenotype/genotype data. 

Before delving into research I worked in Mercedes-Benz Research & Development India (MBRDI) involving myself in stand alone tool development with Qt C++ and python scripting for automation. During my Master's course in JU, I worked on pulmonary artery-vein segmentation from 3D CTA images. Also developed a tool in Qt C++ for the same.

Being a passionate techie I strive to learn and stay updated with the ever changing landscape of Computer Science especially in this era of booming AI. I look for opportunities to put my skills to use and have freelancing experience on developing and hosting a website for a canadian client. I was a Microsoft Student Partner during my undergrad years, which helped me to gain experience in developing Windows phone 8 and Windows Store apps. I am interested in a challenging career in Computer Vision that will continuously motivate me to learn and develop advanced solutions to contemporary problems.
